EMPOWERING THE NATION

Take your domestic skills to another level

Empowering the Nation is a local initiative founded by Precious Radebe to uplift domestic workers and gardeners by providing essential skills training to help them advance professionally. This training school offers specialized programs that equip individuals with a wide range of skills, from first aid and cooking to child minding and garden maintenance. Empowering the Nation not only aims to strengthen individual skills but also to support community growth and economic empowerment.
